Extension Methods

Premium methods,
tailored to you.

01

Sew-In Wefts

Wefts are sewn into a beaded foundation with weight distributed evenly across the entire head, reducing tension and damage. Adjust every 4-8 weeks. Durable enough to reuse for up to a year.

02

Keratin Bonds (K-Tip)

Individual strand-by-strand extensions attached with a professional heat tool. Allows 360-degree mobility for the most natural feel. Typically last 3-5 months with proper care.

03

Hair Tinsel

Ultra-fine metallic threads hand-tied to individual hairs for subtle sparkle without weight. Lasts months through washing and styling. Available in many colors with strategic placement options.
