EXTENSION METHODS

Premium, natural-looking methods
tailored to you.

01

Sew in Wefts

Wefts are sewn into a beaded foundation with weight evenly distributed across the entire head, reducing tension and damage. Adjust every 4-8 weeks. Wefts are durable and can be reused for up to a year.

02

Keratin Bonds (K-Tip)

Individual strand-by-strand extensions attached with a professional heat tool. Allows 360-degree mobility for the most natural feel. Typically last 3-5 months with proper care.

03

Hair Tinsel

Ultra-fine metallic threads hand-tied to individual hairs for subtle sparkle without weight. Lasts months through washing and styling. Available in many colors with strategic placement options.

FAQS

Hair Extension FAQs

How do I choose the best type of extensions for me? +

During your consultation, I'll assess your hair type, lifestyle, and goals to recommend the best extension method for you.

Can I remove extensions myself? +

Please don't! Removing extensions on your own can damage your natural hair. Let me handle the process professionally.

How do I maintain my hair extensions? +

Come in for regular scheduled maintenance and use professional products at home. Brush regularly, keep extensions moisturized, and sleep with a satin pillowcase and bonnet. I'll give you a detailed haircare routine tailored to your extensions. See the Hair Extension Care Guide.

Will hair extensions damage my natural hair? +

When installed and cared for correctly, extensions should not damage your natural hair. I prioritize safe techniques and proper application to ensure hair health. Extensions should be comfortable at all times with no excess tension causing damage.
