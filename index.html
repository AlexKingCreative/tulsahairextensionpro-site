EXTENSION TYPES

Premium, natural-looking methods tailored to your hair, lifestyle, and goals.

Sew in Wefts

Wefts are sewn into a beaded foundation. The weight is evenly distributed across the entire head, reducing the risk of tension and damage. We can install several rows for maximum impact, or we can keep it natural. Wefts should be adjusted every 4-8 weeks. Wefts are durable and can be reused for up to a year.

Keratin Bonds (K-Tip) Extensions

Keratin bonds are individual strand by strand extensions attached using a professional heat tool that safely melts a keratin bond extension to your natural hair. This method allows 360 degree mobility for the most natural feel. They typically last 3-5 months with proper care.

Hair Tinsel

Hair tinsel is a lightweight, strand-by-strand glitter application where ultra-fine metallic threads are hand-tied to individual hairs to add subtle sparkle without weight. Tinsel can lasts for months through washing and styling. I offer many colors and strategic placement options.

FAQS

Hair Extension FAQs

How do I choose the best type of extensions for me? +

During your consultation, I'll assess your hair type, lifestyle, and goals to recommend the best extension method for you.

Can I remove extensions myself? +

Please don't! Removing extensions on your own can damage your natural hair. Let me handle the process professionally.

How do I maintain my hair extensions? +

Please come in for your regular scheduled maintenance appointments and use professional products at home. Brush your hair regularly and keep the extensions moisturized. Sleep with a satin pillow case and bonnet. I'll prescribe you a detailed haircare routine and aftercare instructions tailored to your extensions. See more details in the Hair Extension Care Guide.

Will hair extensions damage my natural hair? +

When installed and cared for correctly, extensions should not damage your natural hair. I prioritize safe techniques and proper application to ensure hair health. Extensions should be comfortable all the time. There should not be any excess tension causing damage.
