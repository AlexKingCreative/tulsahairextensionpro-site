Hair Extension Inquiry

The first step to your dream hair

Welcome! This quick inquiry form is the first step in the hair extension process. Because hair extensions are a highly customized service, we start by learning about your natural hair, hair history, goals, and lifestyle to determine the best method for you.

Once your form is submitted, Hope personally reviews each inquiry to determine if hair extensions are a good fit and which extension method may be most appropriate.

If approved, the next step is scheduling an in-person consultation. During the consultation we will:

  • Evaluate your natural hair
  • Discuss your desired length, fullness, and overall goals
  • Color match your extensions
  • Determine the exact amount of hair needed
  • Create a customized extension plan

Consultations typically take about 30 minutes.

Please upload photos of your current hair and the hair you want - you can email these to HopeNewnam@gmail.com after submitting.
